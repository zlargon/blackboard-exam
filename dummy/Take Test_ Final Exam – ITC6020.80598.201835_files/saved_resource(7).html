Disruptive technology is the breakthrough technology that may change the way how the original industry works. For example, Blockchain technology can be considered as a disruptive technology in the field of virtual currency, such as Bitcoin, that it can complete the transaction without the third-party, trustworthy institution. Blockchain technology stores all transactions in the public ledger, and people can run the software of Bitcoin client and maintain a part of the ledger in Bitcoin system. If anyone wants to alter the ledger, other clients will discover abnormal transaction records and ban the transaction; in this way, Bitcoin can promise that all transactions are legal. Instead of trusting the central institution, Blockchain utilizes cryptography to build a trustworthy system.
