The value creation is the profit we made when an organization deliver a product or a service to customers; in other words, Total Value Created (TVC) = Customer Willingness to Pay (CWP) - Supplier Opportunity Cost (SOC). If the CWP is equal to or less than SOC, the value of TVC will be negative. According to this mathematical formula, we can simply create the value when the CWP is more than SOC.
