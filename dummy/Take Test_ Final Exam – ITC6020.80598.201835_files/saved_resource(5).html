An organization could take a lot of advantages from integrating the information system. First, the integrated information system can offer the greater productivity. The resources of all departments are distributed to avoid the redundant resource, and the workflow will be clear and efficient. Second, the integrated information system can reduce the risk of errors. The data replication to the different location might cause the information not synchronous;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;; however, the integrated information system can reduce the errors and keep the information in real time.
