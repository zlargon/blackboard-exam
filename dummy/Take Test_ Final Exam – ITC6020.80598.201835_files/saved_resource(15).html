Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a strategy to manage the relationship between an organization and its customers. In the enterprise system, compared with the supply chain management (SCM) which focus on the product inventory and delivery as the back-office system, CRM plays the role of the front-office system which includes the several departments, such as marketing, sales, and customer management. The value of the CRM is to understand their customers and get the feedback from them so that the organization would know how to improve their products.
