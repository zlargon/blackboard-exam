Information Technology accelerates the process of exchanging the business information. Although we should not consider IT as the core of a company, IT is definitely the backbone to run an organization. For example, the employees would have to do the paperwork all the time without IT, which is slow and fills with man-made error; instead, the company can utilize the information technology to upload all the business information to the database of the internal system, so that employees can access the database for the information immediately.
