The constant reevaluation of the information system is significant to an organization for two reasons. First, the business strategy and the external environment would change over time, which means the information system is not designed “one and for all.” According to the changes, the information system should upgrade so that it can fit the current business situation properly. Second, a company should reevaluate the information system so that it keeps the competitiveness from new or potential rivals who might devise the business strategy to hit into the market.
